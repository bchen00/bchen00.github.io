About Me

I am currently an undergraduate at University of Pennsylvania studying Physics and Computer Science (expected B.A. and M.S. in Spring 2022). I have conducted my undergraduate research in cryo-electron tomography advised by Prof. Yi-Wei Chang (lab website). The focus of my research has been on developing novel computational systems and chemical imaging methods for cryogenic electron tomography.

In Fall 2022, I will begin a PhD in Electrical Engineering at Caltech where I will be advised by Prof. Mohammad Mirhosseini (lab website). I will be developing chip-scale quantum devices with applications to quantum computing and communication.
