About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering PhD student at Caltech where I am advised by Prof. Mohammad Mirhosseini (lab website). My research is on developing on-chip microwave-optical quantum transducers, with the ultimate goal of enabling distributed quantum computing and long-distance quantum networks. I am supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.


I obtained a B.A. in Physics and Computer Science and M.S. in Physics from University of Pennsylvania in Spring 2022. My undergraduate research was in cryo-electron tomography advised by Prof. Yi-Wei Chang (lab website). The focus of my research was on developing novel computational systems and chemical imaging methods for cryo-electron tomography.
